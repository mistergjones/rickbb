Being Prepared. Helps you in long run.

Whether you’re in the Melbourne Remand Centre, A, B, or C category prisons, I can prepare you for what to expect, the decisions you will need to make, and how to act.

I can’t prepare you for every scenario but I can make you aware through my experiences about what can and will happen along your journey through the system. You need to be smart; you need to be thinking ahead of what’s coming next; and you need to be ready to act.

And when you’re ready to reintegrate into society, I will be there to walk you through “coming out". My reality-based course offer powerful insights and tools to prepare you for prison—and your re-entry into society when you’ve finished your time.

My Experience. Learn from me.

I was sentenced to 5 on the ‘top’ and 3 on the ‘bottom’.
If you understand that sentence, you are already on your way to learning the language of prison. I knew I was going to jail and I just wanted the ‘clock’ to start ticking. I didn’t know what was going to happen to me.

I asked my lawyer, and all he knew was that each time I moved it would get better (Mostly correct). I ended up finding someone recently released asked heaps of questions.
I’m the type of person that wants to be prepared. Hope for the best and expect the worse is a phrase you will hear continuously in prison.
